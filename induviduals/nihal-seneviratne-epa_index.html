Nihal Seneviratne Epa

Nihal Seneviratne Epa is the Chairperson, as well as a Director and shareholder at ASSET Radio Broadcasting Company (Pvt) Limited, where EPA Holdings (Pvt) Limited is the only other shareholder. He owns 38.67 percent of individual shares at ASSET Radio Broadcasting (Pvt) Limited while EPA Holdings (Pvt) Limited owns 61.33 percent of shares. However, Nihal is the sole shareholder of EPA Holdings (Pvt) Limited. As such, he remains the overall owner of ASSET Radio Broadcasting (Pvt) Limited. In 2011, Nihal and Milroy Pieris, a Director of Neth FM, were reportedly legally challenged by Lak Asia Holdings (Pvt) Limited and Sanka Kanchana Wasala Liyanage for preventing them from becoming Directors in ASSET Radio Broadcasting (Pvt) Limited. Nihal Seneviratne Epa also authored a book on Sri Lankan foreign policy. He is a member of the Chamber of Young Lankan Entrepreneurs.
